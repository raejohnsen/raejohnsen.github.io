THE HELVETICA ARCHIVE

By Max Miedinger with art direction by Eduard Hoffmann at Haas. Design began in 1956; first shown as Neue Haas Grotesk in 1957. Revised at Stempel and released for the Linotype as Helvetica in 1959 [Kupferschmid].

Adopted by Letraset in 1964, initially as New Haas Grotesque in two weights.

Revised at Linotype in 1982 as Neue Helvetica (more systematic) and again at Monotype in 2019 as Helvetica Now (three size-specific subfamilies).
